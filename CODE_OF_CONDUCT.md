# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

Contributors are expected to keep discussion respectful, constructive, and focused on improving the project.

Examples of welcome behaviour:

- Being considerate of different experience levels.
- Giving actionable feedback.
- Respecting privacy and security concerns.
- Assuming good intent while staying clear about technical risks.

Examples of unacceptable behaviour:

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Encouraging misuse of transport APIs or user location data.
- Spam or disruptive issue activity.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that violate this code of conduct. Repeated or severe violations may lead to being blocked from the project.
